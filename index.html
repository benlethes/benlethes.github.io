StyleGAN2 Music Performance

EMIK 2022 - Culture.Room Exhibition Opening

I had the privilege of performing the opening of the multidisciplinary exhibition EMIK organized by the international artist network Culture.Room. In my performance, I employed NVIDIA's StyleGAN2 to train a generative image model using the showcased artworks.

Using this model, I generated thousands of frames within the model space, eventually compiling them into a video file. To make the video audio reactive, I used TouchDesigner and synchronized it with various modern compositions from the 20th century that I played on the piano.

Technologies Used

  • NVIDIA StyleGAN2 for generative image synthesis
  • TouchDesigner for audio-reactive visuals
  • Python for model training and frame generation
  • Live piano performance of 20th century compositions

Social Bots & Political Elections

Bachelor's Thesis - WU Vienna

My bachelor's thesis explores how social bots influence opinions during elections, discussing challenges in detecting bots on social media and highlighting their consistent tactics across regions.

Key Findings

  • Detection methods face ongoing challenges due to evolving bot sophistication
  • Bots employ amplification, manipulation of popularity indicators, and human mimicry
  • Psychological effects include the Spiral of Silence and Illusory Truth Effect
  • AI and LLMs pose increasing challenges for bot detection

Research Gap

The thesis identifies a need for more research establishing clearer connections between social bot behavior and direct impact on political discourse, especially given continuous advancements in AI technology.

Arbitrary Relevance

LEER Studio Berlin, August 2022

A data visualization art project created for a spontaneous exhibition at LEER Studio Berlin. The artworks visualize water table levels from 30 monitoring stations in the Berlin area.

Concept

Being familiar with the region where I grew up, I was concerned about the decreasing water levels and sought to explore the artistic potential of environmental data.

Process

  • Scraped data from 30 water table monitoring stations
  • Transformed values to grayscale (0-255 range)
  • Generated two cotton prints: one showing unique values, another highlighting differences
  • Exhibited alongside works by Livia Avianus, Rupert Buesst, Lilith Krauss, and Yan Stolze

Rebranding Alexanderplatz

Final Project - University of Arts Berlin

A conceptual framework for a temporary exhibition showcasing the transformation of Berlin's Alexanderplatz through colored pathways connecting historical, present, and future architectural elements.

The Framework

  • Blue Line: Historical Buildings
  • Turquoise Line: Present Buildings
  • Green Line: Future Buildings (including eight 130m towers)
  • Pink Line: Ways to participate in urban planning processes

Implementation

The design proposed using colored tape for flexibility and easy removal without discoloration, creating an accessible and informative public exhibition about urban development and civic participation.

Improvisation with Merlin Rainer

EMIK 2022 Performance

A collaborative improvisational performance combining visual art, sound, and live music in real-time dialogue.

Performance Setup

Merlin Rainer utilized a canvas with heavy paper and a microphone, making the brushstrokes audible. The painting process was live-streamed onto a wall, creating a visual and sonic interplay.

Musical Approach

  • Piano improvisation inspired by György Ligeti's Études pour piano
  • Original compositions responding to visual brushwork
  • Percussive elements created by audible brush strokes
  • Dynamic dialogue between piano and visual art

The Silence

Natalie Sarraute | Directed by Dávid Paška

A theatre production exploring the power of silence and what lies beneath conventional conversation. Selected for the prestigious Körber Festival Junge Regie in Hamburg (June 2022).

The Play

Jean Pierre remains silent. What initially appears to be a peculiar quirk develops an unexpectedly profound impact on society, challenging conversation norms and compelling others to reveal more about themselves than they intend.

My Role as Director's Assistant

  • Participated in every production phase from script to stage design
  • Coordinated rehearsals and managed stage operations
  • Supported artistic direction and conceptual development

Contemporary Relevance

The production served as a critique of our era of content devoid of substance, where discourse remains inseparable from class thinking and ideological positioning.
