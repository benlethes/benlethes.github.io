StyleGAN2 Music Performance

EMIK 2022 — Culture.Room Exhibition Opening

I performed the opening of the multidisciplinary EMIK exhibition organized by the artist network Culture.Room. Using NVIDIA's StyleGAN2, I trained a generative image model on the showcased artworks, generated thousands of frames, and made the resulting video audio-reactive via TouchDesigner — synchronized with 20th century compositions I played live on piano.

Technologies

  • NVIDIA StyleGAN2 for generative image synthesis
  • TouchDesigner for audio-reactive visuals
  • Python for model training and frame generation
  • Live piano performance of 20th century compositions

Arbitrary Relevance

LEER Studio Berlin, August 2022

A data visualization art project visualizing water table levels from 30 monitoring stations in the Berlin area, created for a spontaneous exhibition at LEER Studio Berlin.

Process

  • Scraped data from 30 water table monitoring stations
  • Transformed values to grayscale (0–255 range)
  • Generated two cotton prints: unique values and level differences
  • Exhibited alongside works by Livia Avianus, Rupert Buesst, Lilith Krauss, and Yan Stolze

Rebranding Alexanderplatz

Final Project — University of Arts Berlin

A conceptual framework for a temporary exhibition presenting the transformation of Berlin's Alexanderplatz through colored pathways connecting historical, present, and future architectural elements.

The Framework

  • Blue Line: Historical Buildings
  • Turquoise Line: Present Buildings
  • Green Line: Future Buildings (including eight 130m towers)
  • Pink Line: Ways to participate in urban planning processes

Improvisation with Merlin Rainer

EMIK 2022 Performance

A collaborative improvisational performance combining visual art, sound, and live music in real-time dialogue. Merlin Rainer used a mic'd canvas, making brushstrokes audible and live-streaming the painting onto a wall. I responded with piano improvisation drawing from György Ligeti's Études pour piano.
